Why I Built PurpleTanuki

Modern kids are drowning in overstimulation. Parents are drowning in guilt. I built PurpleTanuki to create emotionally safe micro-learning rituals that help kids grow without pressure — and help parents feel good about screen time.

The Tanuki is a gentle companion who teaches kids in tiny bursts, builds habits through rituals, and makes learning feel like play.
